Bespoke Fit & Craftsmanship

Precision cut, slow sewing, hand stitching

Our craftsmanship focuses on preserving traditional tailoring handwork. We reject fast fashion production lines in favor of dedicated drafting and precision sewing. Every pattern is cut by hand, and every seam is finished with blind stitches to maximize comfort, resilience, and silhouette flow.

Atelier Tailoring Benchmarks:

  • Handcut Paper Blocks: Drafted specifically from your initial measurements to outline the optimal pattern drape.
  • Silk Thread Seams: We sew critical stress points using heavy silk thread to secure movement flexibility.
  • Double Fused Linings: Interlinings structured using premium organic canvas instead of synthetic glues, preventing bubble distortion on cleaning.

Our Atelier Heritage

Est. 2026 - Independent, premium, design focused

Founded in 2026 as NovaThread (incorporating the vision of Aina Clothing Atelier), our brand was created to make luxury bespoke apparel accessible. We believe that everyone deserves structured patterns that enhance their figure and reflect their lifestyle choices.

Our Brand Core Values:

  • Inclusive Silhouettes: Designing modest and tailored options that make everyone feel respected and stylized.
  • Eco-Responsible Milling: Over 80% of our fabrics are sourced from eco-conscious weaving houses.
  • Apprenticeship Preservation: Supporting local pattern drafters and young tailor interns to preserve sewing arts.
