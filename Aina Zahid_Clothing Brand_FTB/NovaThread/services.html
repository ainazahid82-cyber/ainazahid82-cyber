Custom Tailoring

Precision, Shape, Comfort, Confidence

Our custom tailoring is founded on drawing an individual draft paper pattern for every single customer. We take over 25 body measurements to construct garments that follow the contours of your figure while enabling natural movement.

Tailoring Features:

  • Individual Draft Patterns: Your unique pattern is stored at our atelier for future wardrobe enhancements.
  • Premium Inner Lining: Bemberg silk and custom jacquard weaves to assure elegant drape and inner smoothness.
  • Reinforced Canvas Construction: Traditional half-canvas or full-canvas construction for long-term shape retention.

Fabric Sourcing

Ethical Sourcing, Premium Feel, Long-lasting Fibers

We source luxury textile varieties from heritage mills worldwide. By working directly with producers, we select materials that prioritize sustainable harvesting, organic farming methods, and skin-friendly natural dyes.

Our Materials Catalog:

  • Pure Italian Merino & Cashmere: Ultra-fine breathable fibers sourced from family-run mills in Biella.
  • Long-Staple Egyptian Cotton: Soft, breathable, and highly durable thread matrices perfect for shirting and light modest wear.
  • Premium Crepe & Heavy Georgette Silks: Flowing, elegant fabrics designed specifically to give elegant structure to luxury modest silhouettes.

Styling Consultation

Wardrobe Alignment, Tone Mapping, Seasonal Planning

Our expert designers help translate your style aspirations into cohesive capsules. Whether you are transitioning to modest couture, designing event wear, or aligning your corporate wardrobe, we provide customized, step-by-step guidance.

Consultation Steps:

  • Personal Profile Analysis: Comprehensive analysis of your body architecture, skin tones, and lifestyle requirements.
  • Wardrobe Auditing: Sorting and planning cohesive combinations to maximize wardrobe utility.
  • Digital Sketching & Boarding: We render visual sketches of your tailored styles before crafting begins.
